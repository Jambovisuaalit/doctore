# SKILL: NFL

> Purpose: Validate quarterback and roster status, weather, schedule, market definition, and model domain before approving NFL moneyline, spread, total, team-total, teaser, or player-prop candidates.

## Supported domains

- Regular-season and playoff markets covered by the model.
- Preseason only with a dedicated preseason model.
- Player props only with a dedicated opportunity and distribution model.
- Teasers only with explicitly modeled teaser probability and price.

Do not infer spread, total, prop, or teaser probability from moneyline probability alone.

## Critical confirmations

```yaml
home_team: stable ID
away_team: stable ID
event_start_at: ISO-8601
venue: string
roof_status: open | closed | unknown | not_applicable
weather_snapshot_at: ISO-8601 | null
starting_qb_home: confirmed identity and status
starting_qb_away: confirmed identity and status
injury_report_at: ISO-8601
active_roster_or_inactives_at: ISO-8601 | null
rest_days_home: integer
rest_days_away: integer
market_includes_overtime: boolean
```

Starting quarterback uncertainty is normally `BLOCKED` for full-game team markets unless the model explicitly produces scenario probabilities.

## Model input audit

Preferred team-market features include:

- opponent-adjusted offensive and defensive efficiency;
- quarterback performance and uncertainty;
- offensive-line and pass-protection state;
- pressure, coverage, and explosive-play matchup features;
- early-down and situation-neutral efficiency;
- special teams where validated;
- rest, travel, time zone, altitude, and short-week effects;
- weather and roof state;
- coaching and personnel changes only when encoded consistently;
- market data only when declared in the model contract.

Avoid manually assigning fixed point values to injuries unless the adjustment is generated by a validated model or scenario framework.

## Injury and inactive validation

Verify especially:

- quarterback;
- offensive line;
- pass rush;
- cornerback and safety groups;
- primary receivers and running backs;
- kicker when relevant to props or key-number pricing.

Final inactive lists may materially change the model. A prediction made before inactives must be refreshed when the model contract requires it.

## Weather validation

For outdoor games collect:

- wind speed and direction;
- precipitation;
- temperature;
- field and roof state;
- measurement timestamp.

Wind and severe weather can invalidate total, passing, kicking, and player-prop predictions. Do not improvise a new probability from a weather heuristic.

## Market-specific checks

### Moneyline

- Confirm overtime inclusion.
- Confirm quarterback status.
- Aggregate correlation with spread and team totals.

### Spread

- Match exact handicap and sign.
- Preserve key-number sensitivity in price-threshold reporting.
- Require a margin-distribution or direct spread model.

### Total

- Match exact total.
- Confirm quarterback, weather, pace, and overtime assumptions.
- Require a total or score-distribution model.

### Player props

- Require expected participation, routes, carries, targets, snaps, or attempts as appropriate.
- Match exact stat definition and settlement.
- Block when role or active status is unresolved.

### Teasers

- Calculate teaser EV from the teased lines and combined price.
- Model dependence between legs.
- Crossing common key numbers is not sufficient evidence of value.

## Red flags

Return `BLOCKED` or `WATCH` when:

- quarterback status changes or is unresolved;
- major offensive-line changes occur after prediction generation;
- severe weather changes beyond model tolerance;
- final inactives materially differ from projected availability;
- the model is regular-season only but the event is preseason;
- market period or overtime settlement is mismatched;
- a prop lacks a current role projection;
- the price moved and no longer clears the threshold.
