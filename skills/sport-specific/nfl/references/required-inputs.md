# NFL required inputs

- season, week, teams, venue, kickoff, surface, roof, and status;
- exact market, line, period, and overtime rules;
- model version, target market, feature cutoff, and calibration status;
- quarterback and backup status;
- offensive-line availability and expected combinations;
- key receiver, running back, tight end, defensive back, pass-rush, and special-teams status when modeled;
- weather, rest, travel, time-zone, and short-week assumptions;
- props: player identity, role, snap share, route share, carries/targets, and void rules.
