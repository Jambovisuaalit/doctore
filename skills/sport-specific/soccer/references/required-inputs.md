# Soccer required inputs

- competition, season, fixture, venue, and kickoff time;
- regulation-time versus qualification or extra-time market definition;
- projected/confirmed lineups and model feature cutoff;
- goalkeeper and key-player availability;
- rest, travel, congestion, rotation, weather, and pitch state when modeled;
- exact market line, period, and settlement rules;
- model domain and calibration for the competition or documented transfer method.
