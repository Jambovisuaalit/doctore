# MLB required inputs

## All markets

- confirmed event, venue, start time, and status;
- model version, feature cutoff, and target market;
- executable odds and synchronized market snapshot;
- listed-pitcher or action settlement rule;
- confirmed or projected starters and lineup state;
- bullpen usage and availability cutoff;
- park, roof status, weather snapshot, and relevant handedness features.

## Totals

Also require run-environment assumptions, wind direction relative to park orientation, temperature, precipitation, roof state, and umpire only if the model uses it.

## First five

Confirm F5 settlement rules and that the model excludes or handles bullpen contributions consistently.
