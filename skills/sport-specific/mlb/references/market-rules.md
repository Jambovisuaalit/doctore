# MLB market rules

- Full-game moneyline may include extra innings; model target must match.
- Run line requires exact handicap and push rules.
- Totals require identical line and settlement rules on both sides.
- F5 markets vary by book on ties, listed pitchers, and void conditions.
- A starter change after feature cutoff is normally BLOCKED until the model refreshes.
- A materially different lineup, roof state, or weather regime is WATCH or BLOCKED depending on model sensitivity and time to start.
