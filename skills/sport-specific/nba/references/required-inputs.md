# NBA/WNBA required inputs

- league, season phase, teams, venue, start time, and status;
- exact market, line, period, and overtime rules;
- model version, target, feature cutoff, and league-specific calibration;
- official/credible injury status, active roster, projected starters, and minutes assumptions;
- rest days, back-to-back, travel, altitude, and pace inputs when modeled;
- for props: player, stat, line, expected role, minutes, and void rules.
