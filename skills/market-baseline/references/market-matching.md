# Market matching

- Moneyline outcomes must share identical overtime, extra-inning, retirement, and listed-participant rules.
- Spread sides must use complementary handicaps on the same line.
- Total sides must use the same total and push treatment.
- 1X2 requires home, draw, and away from the same market.
- Asian quarter-lines require settlement-aware handling; do not treat them as ordinary binary outcomes without decomposing the stake.
- Player props must share the same player, stat, line, period, and void rules.
