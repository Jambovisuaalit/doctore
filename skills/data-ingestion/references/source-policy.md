# Source policy

## Priority

1. Official league, team, tournament, venue, or sportsbook source.
2. Licensed odds or sports-data feed with timestamps.
3. Established statistical provider.
4. Verified beat reporter or event reporter for late status changes.
5. Secondary aggregator only as corroboration.

## Reconciliation

- Prefer the newest authoritative source.
- Preserve conflicts instead of silently choosing a convenient value.
- Record both source timestamps and retrieval timestamps.
- Do not use search-result snippets as final evidence when the underlying source is available.
- Do not infer cause from line movement unless a reliable source states it.
