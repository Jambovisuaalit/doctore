# Bias checklist

- Forced action: Is a bet being selected because the user expects daily volume?
- Recency: Is the case driven by the last game or short streak beyond the model design?
- Result chasing: Is stake or selection influenced by recent losses?
- Favorite or underdog identity: Is team reputation replacing price analysis?
- Price anchoring: Is an old or unavailable price influencing the decision?
- Confirmation: Were contradictory injuries, lineups, or market signals ignored?
- Public fade: Is public percentage treated as evidence without a causal model?
- Sharp-money story: Is line movement being assigned an unsupported cause?
- Double counting: Is context already captured by the model being manually added again?
- Outcome bias: Is a previous win or loss being used to judge process quality?
